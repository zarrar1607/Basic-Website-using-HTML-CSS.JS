Welcome to Muffakham Jah College of Engineering and Technology

Muffakham Jah College of Engineering and Technology (MJCET) was established in the year 1980 by Sultan-ul-Uloom Education Society (SUES) which is formed by a group of visionaries and intellectuals from various walks of life. Today that tiny acorn has developed into a mighty oak. The college is affiliated to Osmania University, Hyderabad and approved by AICTE, New Delhi.
